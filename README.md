## Loan Performance (Servicing)
#### loan_sequence_number – Unique identifier for each loan (merge key)
#### monthly_reporting_period – Month the loan was reported
#### current_actual_upb – Remaining unpaid loan balance
#### current_loan_delinquency_status – Current delinquency/payment status
#### loan_age – Loan age in months
#### remaining_months_to_legal_maturity – Months remaining until maturity
#### modification_flag – Indicates if the loan has been modified (keeping for now)
#### zero_balance_code – Reason loan balance became zero (keeping for now)
#### zero_balance_effective_date – Date the loan reached a zero balance (keeping for now)
#### current_interest_rate – Current mortgage interest rate
#### current_deferred_upb – Deferred unpaid principal balance
#### due_date_of_last_paid_installment – Last payment due date (keeping for now)
#### step_modification_flag – Indicates a step-rate modification (keeping for now)
#### deferred_payment_plan – Indicates whether borrower is on a deferred payment plan (keeping for now)
#### estimated_loan_to_value – Estimated current loan-to-value ratio
#### zero_balance_removal_upb – Principal balance removed when loan closed (keeping for now)
#### delinquency_due_to_disaster – Indicates disaster-related delinquency (keeping for now)
#### borrower_assistance_status_code – Borrower assistance/loss mitigation status (keeping for now)
#### interest_bearing_upb – Portion of balance that accrues interest
